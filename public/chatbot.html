Frequently Asked Questions

What types of e-waste can I recycle?

You can recycle various electronic devices including smartphones, laptops, tablets, desktop computers, televisions, printers, and household electronics. Our AI can help you classify your specific device.

How do I prepare my e-waste for pickup?

Remove personal data, disconnect all cables, and place devices in a secure container. For batteries, ensure they're properly packaged to prevent damage during transport.

Is my data safe when recycling?

Yes! We follow strict data security protocols. All devices are securely wiped or destroyed during the recycling process to protect your personal information.
