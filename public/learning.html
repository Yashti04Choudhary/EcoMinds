Did You Know?

Recycling one million laptops saves the energy equivalent to the electricity used by 3,657 US homes in a year.

E-waste represents 2% of America's trash in landfills, but it equals 70% of overall toxic waste.

For every one million cell phones that are recycled, 35,274 lbs of copper, 772 lbs of silver, 75 lbs of gold, and 33 lbs of palladium can be recovered.
