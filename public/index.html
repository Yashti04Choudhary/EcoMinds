Our Mission

EcoMind AI is dedicated to revolutionizing e-waste management through intelligent technology. We bridge the gap between environmental consciousness and practical action, making recycling accessible and rewarding for everyone.

We dream of a cleaner India where every smartphone, laptop, and gadget finds a responsible second life. EcoMinds empowers you to take action—transforming electronic waste from a growing problem into a sustainable opportunity for our planet.


Our Intuition

India is one of the world's top e-waste generators, with 2 million tons produced every year—and most of it isn't recycled safely. Rapid tech growth, low awareness, and limited access to recycling centers make responsible disposal a real challenge. EcoMinds was created by students, for students and communities, to bridge this gap and make recycling easy and accessible for everyone.

Government Initiatives We Support

Extended Producer Responsibility
